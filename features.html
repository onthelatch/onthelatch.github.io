App Features

Scan

Scan tickets or digital passes quickly and with ease using the camera on your Android device.

Scan Report

Get all the information you need about capacities and scan counts at a glance in the Scan Report.

Attendee List

Patron lost their ticket? Look them up by name or seat number on the door, and manually admit them without needing to visit the Box Office for a reprint.

Access Notes

Powered by Ticket Attributes, give your team the information they need to ensure patrons with accessibility needs are anticipated and prepared for.

Group Scan Mode

Enable Group Scan mode to automatically scan all tickets in a group just from one ticket!

Scan Speed

Scanning speed can be adjusted to suit your ushers and to match their comfort-level.
